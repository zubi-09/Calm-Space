😔 PTSD & Trauma Support

PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder) can occur after experiencing or witnessing a life-threatening or deeply distressing event. Trauma impacts both emotional and physical well-being, often leading to anxiety, flashbacks, and avoidance behaviors.

Common Symptoms

  • Flashbacks or intrusive memories
  • Nightmares and sleep issues
  • Emotional numbness or detachment
  • Hypervigilance or extreme alertness

How to Cope

  • Practice grounding and mindfulness exercises
  • Talk to a trauma-informed therapist
  • Engage in calming routines (yoga, journaling)
  • Avoid self-blame and be kind to yourself

When to Get Help

If symptoms persist for more than a month or interfere with your daily life, don’t hesitate to reach out to a licensed mental health professional for support and treatment.
