⚡ Understanding Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder causes extreme mood swings including emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression). It’s a lifelong condition but manageable with treatment.

Common Symptoms

  • Periods of high energy and impulsivity
  • Episodes of deep sadness or hopelessness
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Changes in sleep patterns

Common Triggers

  • Stressful life events
  • Sleep disruption
  • Substance abuse
