💔 Suicide & Self-Harm Support

When emotions feel too heavy to carry, thoughts of self-harm or suicide can appear. You're not alone — reaching out is a sign of strength, not weakness. Support is always available, and things can get better.

Warning Signs

  • Talking about feeling hopeless or trapped
  • Withdrawing from friends and activities
  • Giving away possessions or saying goodbye
  • Engaging in risky or self-destructive behavior

What You Can Do

  • Talk to someone you trust — a friend, mentor, or counselor
  • Reach out to a crisis line or therapist immediately
  • Use grounding exercises to stay present
  • Make a safety plan with emergency contacts and coping tools
