🔄 Understanding ADHD

ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder) is a neurodevelopmental condition that affects attention, impulse control, and energy levels. It’s common in children but can continue into adulthood, influencing daily life.

Common Symptoms

  • Difficulty sustaining attention
  • Impulsiveness
  • Hyperactivity or restlessness
  • Disorganization

Common Triggers

  • Stressful or chaotic environments
  • Lack of structure or routine
  • Sleep deprivation
