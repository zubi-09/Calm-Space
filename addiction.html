🔗 Understanding Addiction

Addiction is a complex condition where someone becomes dependent on substances or behaviors despite negative consequences. It’s not a matter of willpower but changes in the brain that make quitting difficult.

Common Signs

  • Intense cravings
  • Loss of control over use
  • Neglecting responsibilities and relationships
  • Withdrawal symptoms when stopping

Common Triggers

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Social pressure
  • Emotional pain or trauma
