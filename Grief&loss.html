💔 Coping with Grief & Loss

Grief is a natural response to losing someone or something important. It’s deeply personal and may involve a wide range of emotions — from sadness and anger to confusion and numbness.

Common Reactions

  • Intense sorrow or yearning
  • Disbelief or shock
  • Anger or guilt
  • Withdrawal from social contact

Coping Strategies

  • Talk to trusted friends or a therapist
  • Allow yourself to grieve without judgment
  • Practice self-care and rest
  • Join a support group

When to Seek Help

If grief becomes overwhelming or prolonged, it may be time to talk with a mental health professional. Complicated grief can affect your daily life and overall well-being.
