🌧️ Understanding Depression

Depression is more than feeling sad; it’s a persistent condition affecting mood, thoughts, and daily activities. It can make even small tasks feel overwhelming.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Loss of interest in hobbies
  • Fatigue or lack of energy
  • Changes in sleep or appetite

Common Triggers

  • Trauma or loss
  • Chronic stress
  • Isolation or loneliness

When to Seek Help
